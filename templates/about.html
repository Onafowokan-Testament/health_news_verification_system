About MedVer

Evidence-based help for spotting common health misinformation — starting from Nigeria-focused myths and trusted sources.

How it works

A simple pipeline built for clarity.

  • Searches a curated Nigerian health myths knowledge base first.
  • Optionally fetches PubMed research when you enable it in configuration.
  • Uses Gemini to produce readable verdicts, explanations, and citations.
  • Supports typed claims and uploaded audio (transcribed before checking).

Safety notice

This tool is informational and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

If symptoms are severe or urgent — chest pain, trouble breathing, heavy bleeding, confusion — seek emergency care immediately.
